Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a leading AI agency, here’s how we got here.

  • 2008 - The Beginning

    Hernan Humana started his career as a software developer at a small tech startup. His early experiences in coding and project management laid the foundation for his future endeavors.

  • 2012 - A New Path

    Nicolas Salas joined the team, bringing his expertise in AI and machine learning. Together, they began working on innovative projects that pushed the boundaries of technology.

  • 2015 - Growth and Expansion

    As the team grew, Felix Espinoza came on board as the lead developer. His skills in building scalable systems and his passion for AI were crucial in developing our core technologies.

  • 2020 - Birth of KÜTRAL

    The trio founded KÜTRAL with the vision of making AI accessible to everyone. They focused on creating personalized AI solutions that could adapt to various needs and industries.

  • 2023 - Leading the Industry

    Today, KÜTRAL is recognized as a leader in the AI industry. Our team continues to innovate and push the limits of what’s possible with AI, driven by our mission to empower businesses and individuals alike.
